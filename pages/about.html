This website is a showcase of John Walton's personal work, giving the viewer both a place to enjoy his perspective & the opportunity to purchase prints.

The image collections reveal the beauty of the urban margins of east London where the city & country collide. Whether social or landscape subject, the dynamic conflict between conservation & construction forms fleeting moments of wonder in a fragile world.

We hope you enjoy the pictures.
